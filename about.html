About Group-2

Our Team

Our group is undertaking a project for the Life Skills and Professional Communication subject, where we are creating a video podcast focused on innovative technological interventions and research in the Kerala context. Through this project, we aim to highlight the impactful contributions made by academicians, professionals, government agencies, research institutions, and NGOs in the field of technology.

We are committed to utilizing effective communication skills, a clear narrative structure, and creative visuals to deliver insightful content. The project encourages us to hone our collaborative, research, communication, and presentation skills, which are essential for both personal and professional development.

Our team of 6 members ,Each member plays a crucial role in ensuring the success of this project: Project leader, Script writer, Narrator ,Designer ,Editor and a Researcher.

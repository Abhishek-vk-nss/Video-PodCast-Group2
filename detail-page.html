IOT In Agriculture

1. Deepflow Technologies Deepflow Technologies provides an end-to-end solution for farm planning and implementation, impacting over 4 million farmers in Kerala. Their app offers tools and knowledge essential for modern farming practices.

2. K-AgTech LaunchPad The Kerala Agricultural University is set to launch the K-AgTech LaunchPad at Vellayani College of Agriculture. This center will support agribusiness development by assisting farmers, FPOs, and self-help groups with customized support throughout their entrepreneurial journey.

3. KATHIR Platform KATHIR is an initiative that integrates advanced technology with local agricultural knowledge to bridge the information gap regarding climate change impacts. It serves as a comprehensive resource for farmers, providing insights into sustainable practices and climate-resilient strategies.
